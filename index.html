Televisions & Energy

Modern TVs in Australia must display an Energy Rating Label. Energy-efficient televisions use less power, saving money and reducing emissions. Larger screens and high brightness settings typically increase energy use.

  • LED TVs are more efficient than plasma.
  • 4K and OLED displays consume more power.
  • Standby mode can still use electricity.
